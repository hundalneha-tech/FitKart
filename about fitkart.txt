FitKart – Shop Fitness. Earn Rewards. Stay Active.

FitKart is a next-generation fitness shopping app built for people who love sports, fitness, and an active lifestyle. It brings together everything you need — from sportswear and footwear to supplements and workout gear — into one seamless shopping experience.

What makes FitKart unique is that it rewards you for staying active.

As you walk, run, or exercise, the app tracks your activity and converts your effort into reward points. These points can then be redeemed as discounts while purchasing sports products like shoes, track pants, T-shirts, yoga mats, supplements, soccer gear, and more.

With FitKart, every step you take helps you save on the gear that keeps you moving.

Whether you're a beginner starting your fitness journey or a sports enthusiast upgrading your gear, FitKart turns your daily exercise into real shopping benefits.

Walk more. Earn more. Shop smarter — with FitKart.
